Welcome to DogBread

Dogbread is a mini project inspired by hit videogame UNDERTALE

In it, you fight Loaf, a sentient Dog-Bread monster from the underground, whose curiosity and puns can break the spirit of even the toughest opponents.

Your goal is to either defeat Loaf

how dare you

or befriend him by enduring his relentless puns and attacks.

Choose your path wisely, as your decisions will shape the outcome of your encounter with Loaf!

Controls:

  • Use Arrow Keys to move the heart (your soul)
  • Press Z to select options during the battle
  • Press X to cancel selections
  • Dodge Loaf's attacks by moving your soul out of harm's way
